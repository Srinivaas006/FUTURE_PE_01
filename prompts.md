# Prompts — AI Website Copy Generator (Task 1)

## Business: CareFirst Clinic, Kakinada

---

## Prompt 1 — Homepage Hero Section

```
You are an expert website copywriter specializing in local business websites.
Write a homepage hero section for a family clinic called "CareFirst Clinic" located in Kakinada, Andhra Pradesh.
The clinic offers same-day appointments, has experienced doctors, and provides affordable care for families, professionals, and senior citizens.
Tone: Warm, trustworthy, and professional.
Include: 
- A headline (max 10 words)
- A subheadline (1-2 sentences)
- A primary CTA button text
- A secondary CTA button text
Do not use clichés like "We care for you." Be specific and human.
```

---

## Prompt 2 — Services Page

```
You are a conversion copywriter for healthcare websites.
Write service descriptions for CareFirst Clinic in Kakinada. 
Services to cover: General Medicine, Pediatrics, Women's Health, and Senior Care.
For each service write:
- A short title (3-5 words)
- A 2-sentence description focusing on patient benefit
- One trust signal (e.g., "Board-certified specialist", "500+ patients served")
Tone: Reassuring and clear. Avoid medical jargon. Keep it simple for a general audience.
```

---

## Prompt 3 — CTA / Contact Section

```
Write a contact and booking Call-To-Action section for CareFirst Clinic.
The section should:
- Address the most common patient hesitation (e.g., "Will I get an appointment today?")
- Reassure them with a quick sentence
- Include a strong CTA headline
- List 3 contact methods: Phone, WhatsApp, and Walk-in address
- Add a closing trust line below the CTA button
Location: Near Town Hall, Kakinada. Phone: [CLINIC_PHONE]. 
Tone: Friendly and action-oriented.
```
